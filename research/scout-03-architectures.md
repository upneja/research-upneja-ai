# Scout 03 — Efficient & Alternative Architectures / ML Systems
_Filed 2026-06-21. VENUE: NeurIPS 2026 = Sydney, Dec 6–12 (corrects brief). Spine: in 2024/25 "the architecture" was settled background (dense/MoE Transformer); IN 2026 THE BACKBONE BECAME CONTESTED AND STARTED SHIPPING. Strongest signal = CONVERGENCE: multiple independent labs (Alibaba/Moonshot/Ai2/NVIDIA/IBM) shipped the same recipe — gated-delta-rule linear attention interleaved w/ full attention ~3:1 + MoE + increasingly 4-bit. Labonne Feb 17: "Nobody Agrees on Attention Anymore." Honest verdict: Transformer NOT replaced, but no longer pure, no longer a monopoly._

## Emerging concepts
1. **Hybrid linear-attention LLMs (delta-rule + attention, ~3:1)** — SURGING. Kimi Linear (2510.26692, KDA, 6× decode @1M, 75% KV cut); Qwen3-Next/Qwen3.5-397B (Gated DeltaNet + gated full attn 3:1, 512 experts); **Olmo Hybrid 7B** (2604.03444, fully open, 3:1 GDN, ~49% fewer tokens); Gated DeltaNet-2 (2605.22791, beats Mamba-3 @1.3B); MiniMax-01 Lightning (2501.08313). Freshness VERY HIGH (Jamba/Zamba were footnotes at NeurIPS24/25; now backbone of multiple frontier open models).
2. **Native FP4 *training* at scale (NVFP4/MXFP4)** — SURGING, VERIFIED. **NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Ultra** (Jun 4, shipped open 550B-total/55B-active MoE hybrid, **pretrained natively in NVFP4 on 20T tokens, <0.4% loss gap vs BF16**); "Pretraining LLMs with NVFP4" (2509.25149, 12B/10T); **Quartet II (2601.22813, ICML 2026)** unbiased micro-scaled gradients; AMD MLPerf v6.0 production MXFP4 (Jun 16); HiFloat4 (2604.08826, Huawei). NUANCE: "FP4 training" = always mixed precision; nobody trains pure-FP4; DeepSeek-V4 FP4 = QAT/post-training not base pretraining. Freshness VERY HIGH (NeurIPS25 frontier was FP8).
3. **Trained-in sparse attention as the real long-context fix (NSA→DSA)** — SURGING. DeepSeek-V4 (CSA + DeepSeek Sparse Attention via FP8 Lightning Indexer top-k≈2048, O(L²)→O(Lk), 1M ctx); Native Sparse Attention (2502.11089); GLM-5 adopted DSA; SGLang/vLLM ship DSA. Freshness HIGH (replaces "scale RoPE + bigger window").
4. **Reasoning-aware KV-cache compression (compress the THOUGHTS not the input)** — SURGING. **ThinKV (2510.01290, ICLR 2026 ORAL)** quant+eviction by thought importance, near-lossless @<5% KV, 5.8× throughput on R1-Distill; R-KV (2505.24133, 90% saving); RLKV (2510.08525). Counter-lit: "Pitfalls of KV Cache Compression" (2510.00231, ACL 2026, silent prompt leakage). Freshness HIGH (category didn't exist in NeurIPS24 baseline; child of o1/R1).
5. **Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) as unified train-and-serve primitive** — SURGING. Draft model disappearing: MTP heads trained into base → self-draft+verify. "MTP via Self-Distillation" (2602.06019, >3× decode <5% GSM8K drop); FastMTP (2509.18362); DeepSeek-V3/V4 + Qwen3.5/3.6 ship native MTP; llama.cpp/SGLang support. Skeptic: "Speculative Decoding: Performance or Illusion?" (2601.11580, gains shrink at production batch sizes). Freshness HIGH.
6. **New MoE science: load-balancing breaks at extreme sparsity + first rigorous theory** — SURGING. Activation ratios collapsed (Mixtral 28% → V3 5.4% → V4 ~3%), experts ballooned (Qwen3-Next 512). Auxiliary-loss-free bias trick **fails in lower layers** at extreme sparsity. **"Theoretical Framework for Auxiliary-Loss-Free Load Balancing" (2512.03915)** — frames as single-step primal-dual assignment, proves log expected-regret bound. LP balancing (2511.16947); MoE scaling laws "Towards Greater Leverage" (2507.17702), "Holistic Scaling Laws for Optimal MoE" (2603.21862); "Optimal Sparsity of MoE for Reasoning" (2508.18672 — reasoning vs memorization want different sparsity). Freshness HIGH ("balancing works" → "balancing provably breaks at the sparsity we ship, here's the math").
7. **Mamba-3 & pure-SSM revival via state-space principles** — SURGING (prestigious). **Mamba-3 (2603.15569, ICLR 2026 ORAL)** — trapezoidal discretization, **complex-valued state updates** (via proven equivalence to data-dependent RoPE), MIMO; +1.8 over Gated DeltaNet @1.5B. Log-Linear Attention (2506.04761, ICLR2026); RWKV-7 Goose (2503.14456, ICLR2026, beyond-TC⁰); xLSTM-7B (2510.02228, ICLR2026). Freshness HIGH (state-tracking + inference-first reframing).

**Calibration (momentum vs hype):** field is policing itself — "The End of Transformers?" (2510.05364) finds NO pure sub-quadratic model competitive at 14–70B, only hybrids compete; MiniMax-M2.5 reverted to plain MHA; DeepSeek-V4 bet on SPARSE not linear/SSM. Accurate claim: "pure-Transformer backbone lost its MONOPOLY; 2026 is an open contest among hybrid-linear, trained-in-sparse, MLA/MHA." VENUE-ROUTING RISK: heavy systems work (serving/kernels) skews MLSys/OSDI/EuroSys, under-indexes at NeurIPS.

## NeurIPS 2026 prediction candidates
- **C1 Hybrid linear-attention LLMs (delta-rule + attention) = recognized standard architecture class.** Novel (footnotes→backbone of multiple frontier open models from independent labs). High-prob (Kimi Linear/Qwen3.5/Olmo Hybrid/GatedDeltaNet-2 + distillation cluster HALO 2601.22156 = exactly the corpus NeurIPS26 cites; cross-lab convergence guarantees follow-ons). **Conf 85%.** Criterion: ≥6 main/spotlight/oral papers on gated-delta-rule/hybrid-linear arch + ≥2 of {Mamba-3, Kimi Linear/KDA, Olmo Hybrid, GatedDeltaNet-2} as named baselines.
- **C2 Native FP4 *training* (NVFP4/MXFP4) = recognized training paradigm.** Novel (NeurIPS25 frontier was FP8; shipped 550B 4-bit-pretrained model + production MLPerf MXFP4 + ≥4 method papers). High-prob (Quartet II ICML2026; 3-vendor silicon NVIDIA/AMD/Huawei; scaling-law+kernels+big-model = NeurIPS shape). **Conf 85%.** Criterion: ≥4 main-proceedings papers w/ FP4/NVFP4/MXFP4 *training* (not inference/PTQ) primary, ≥1 at ≥7B/≥1T tokens.
- **C3 Reasoning-aware KV-cache compression (compress the CoT).** Novel (target shifts input ctx → generated reasoning traces; child of o1/R1). High-prob (ThinKV ICLR2026 Oral + R-KV/RLKV; reasoning models dominant going in). **Conf 80%.** Criterion: ≥4 accepts w/ KV compression specifically for reasoning/CoT/long-generation + ≥1 spotlight/oral or session.
- **C4 Theory + practice of MoE load balancing under extreme sparsity.** Novel (newly-created failure mode of standard DeepSeek recipe; "we finally understand the trick everyone copied"). High-prob (every lab ships <5%-activation regime triggering bug; theory+LP+routers converging). **Conf 72%.** Criterion: ≥2 main-track papers w/ MoE load-balancing/routing under high sparsity central (new theory or replacement motivated by lower-layer collapse).
- **C5 "Effective context" / honest long-context evaluation as benchmark category.** Novel (reframes long-context capability→measurement; thesis: "long-context NOT solved in 2026 — the year the field stopped pretending"). High-prob (NoLiMa 2502.05167 GPT-4o 99.3%→69.7% @32K; Chroma Context Rot all 18 degrade; RULER 50–65% effective; NeurIPS ED track natural home). **Conf 70%.** Criterion: ≥3 papers introducing/extending reasoning-over-long-context / effective-context benchmarks critiquing NIAH, "context rot"/"effective context length" as named concepts.

**Honorable mentions:** LLM-generated GPU kernels (FlashInfer-Bench 2601.00227) surging but skews MLSys; heterogeneous experts (Grove MoE 2508.07785); sub-4-bit/ternary (ParetoQ 2502.02631) high count but reads as 2024 continuation.

**Caveats:** (1) NeurIPS taxonomy may file under "efficient LLMs"/"model architecture"/"long-context" not clean "SSM/linear-attention" label → phrase criteria by concept not session name. (2) DeepSeek-V4 param counts / vendor throughput / leaderboard % from analyst blogs+model cards → cite as "reported."
