# Scout 05 — Post-Training, RL & Alignment for LLMs
_Filed 2026-06-21. Baseline was "RLHF + PPO + DPO + reward model." By mid-2026 the stack rotated: PPO gone from frontier; debate = which critic-free group-relative successor wins, does RL teach vs elicit, how to push verifiable-reward RL into no-verifier domains._

## Emerging concepts
1. **Post-GRPO algorithm shakeout (sequence-level & token-decoupled PO)**. GRPO now baseline-to-beat. **DAPO** (clip-higher, dynamic sampling, token-level; AIME'24 50pts @~50% GRPO steps); **GSPO** Group SEQUENCE Policy Optimization (Qwen, 2507.18071) — sequence-level importance ratio, fixes MoE RL collapse, trained Qwen3-235B-A22B (most-cited algorithmic correction of cycle); 2026 hybrids: Dynamic Hybrid PO for RLVR (2601.05607), "GRPO Policy Gradient is a U-Statistic" (2603.01162), Sharpness-Guided GRPO (2511.00066). **SURGING.** Freshness HIGH (PPO-vs-DPO → which GRPO-successor + granularity; MoE-stability now first-class).
2. **"Elicit vs teach" reasoning debate (empirical program)**. Elicit/ceiling camp: "Does RL Incentivize Reasoning Beyond Base Model?" (2504.13837, Limit-of-RLVR), Invisible Leash (2507.14843). Teach/expand camp: **ProRL** (2505.24864, NeurIPS 2025 poster), "New Skills or Sharper Primitives?" (2602.08281). Mechanism papers: Learning Dynamics at Edge of Competence (2602.14872), Unlearnability in RLVR (2605.16787), RL Grokking Recipe (2509.21016). **SURGING.** Freshness VERY HIGH (barely existed pre-R1; now the field's central scientific question about RL).
3. **RLVR beyond verifiable domains — "the verifier problem" / rubric & generative rewards** (HOTTEST single cluster, the big EXPANSION story). **Rubrics-as-Rewards** (2507.17746, +31% HealthBench); 2026 sub-lit: Alternating RL rubric (2602.01511), Data-driven rubrics (2602.06795), Robust Rubric Rewards (2605.30244); verifier-free: **RLPR** (2506.18254), Compute as Teacher (2509.14234), Judge Code (ICLR 2026); OpenReview track "All in RLVR on Non-Verifiable Domains." **SURGING.** Freshness HIGH.
4. **Self-play / self-evolving with asymmetric proposer-solver curricula ("zero-data" RL)**. Absolute Zero Reasoner (2505.03335) → **R-Zero** (2508.05004) → **SPICE** Self-Play In Corpus Environments (2510.24684, corpus-grounded prevents hallucination drift, +8.9% math). 2026: Scaling Self-Play w/ Self-Guidance (2604.20209), Edge of Learnability (2601.18778), Multi-Agent Evolve (2510.23595); skeptic: R-Diverse "diversity illusion" (2602.13103). **SURGING + real skeptic line.** Freshness VERY HIGH.
5. **Long-horizon credit assignment for LLM agents (turn-level/hierarchical/hindsight)** — freshest cluster, Q2-2026 heavy. "From Reasoning to Agentic: Credit Assignment in RL for LLMs" (2604.09459); HiPER (2602.16165); **HCAPO** Hindsight Credit Assignment (2603.08754, +7.7% WebShop/+13.8% ALFWorld over GRPO); Turn-PPO; Co-Evolution of Policy & Internal Reward (2604.03098); Rethinking Agentic RL (2604.27859). **SURGING.** Freshness HIGH (agentic credit assignment as named subfield is new).
6. **Reward hacking as formal phenomenon + RM robustness**. "Reward Hacking in the Era of Large Models" (2604.13602, formalizes); SignCert-PO (2604.02986); Bayesian Non-negative RM (2602.10623); info-theoretic RM (2510.13694). **STEADY→SURGING**, safety-adjacent. Freshness MED.
7. **Unsupervised / intrinsic-reward RL ("1-example / entropy-only" surprises)**. RL with One Training Example (2504.20571, MATH500 36→73.6%); Entropy Minimization (2505.15134); 2026 "when/why": Manifold Envelopment (2603.16578). **STEADY + HYPE caveat** (Qwen-math priors; headline numbers skeptical).

## NeurIPS 2026 prediction candidates
- **P1 RLVR beyond verifiable domains via rubric/generative verifiers = headline reward-modeling story.** Novel (synthesis, phrase didn't exist in baseline). High-prob (densest 2026 cluster, clear lineage, benchmark wins, dedicated OpenReview track, universal lab adoption pressure). **Conf 80%.** Criterion: ≥5 papers w/ rubric/generative/programmatic verifiers for non-verifiable RL + ≥1 spotlight/oral or workshop.
- **P2 "Does RL teach or elicit reasoning?" = marquee scientific thread.** Novel (mechanism question, not a model; reviewers love unresolved falsifiable controversy). High-prob (two formed camps, ProRL NeurIPS2025, compounding Feb–May 2026 mechanism papers). **Conf 70%.** Criterion: ≥4 papers engaging "expand vs sharpen" via pass@k/boundary/dynamics + ≥1 spotlight/oral or panel.
- **P3 Long-horizon credit assignment for LLM agents.** Novel (agentic credit assignment as RL subfield). High-prob (freshest Q2-2026 momentum, gains over GRPO, obvious next problem, May-2026 submissions in window). **Conf 68% (dark horse).** Criterion: ≥4 papers on credit assignment/advantage estimation for multi-turn agents.
- **P4 Self-evolving proposer-solver self-play, corpus-grounded favored.** Novel (model manufactures own curriculum). High-prob (AZR→R-Zero→SPICE lineage, "running out of data" narrative, built-in skeptic lit). **Conf 62% (hype risk priced by own skeptic papers).** Criterion: ≥4 self-play/self-evolving curricula papers + ≥1 spotlight OR workshop.
- **P5 (lower) MoE-stable/sequence-level RL (GSPO-lineage) = de facto default + reward-hacking robustness companion.** **Conf 55%** (risk: infrastructure-assumed not spotlighted). Criterion: seq-level/MoE-stable RL named in ≥3 papers + cited as default + ≥3 reward-hacking papers.

**Cross-cutting skeptic notes:** (a) many gains on Qwen-math bases — generality contested. (b) "zero-data self-play" + "1-example RL" = highest hype-risk; 2026 follow-ups already walking back universality. (c) safest bets (P1,P2) = problem framings w/ two-sided literatures (survive review better than "method X wins").
