# Scout 01 — LLM Reasoning & Test-Time Compute
_Filed 2026-06-21. "Reasoning/test-time compute" itself = baseline, NOT novel. Novelty = where the frontier MOVED in 2026._

## Emerging concepts
- **A1. Latent / looped reasoning at scale** (recurrent-depth, weight-shared "thinking in hidden space"; scale compute by DEPTH not tokens). Anchor: **Ouro "Scaling Latent Reasoning via Looped Language Models"** (2510.25741, Oct 2025) — 1.4B/2.6B looped, 7.7T tokens, 2.6B rivals 8B+; big community traction, open-sourced. Follow-on cluster Feb–May 2026: Loop Think & Generalize (2604.07822), Reward Latent Thought Trajectories (2602.10520), Mechanistic Analysis of Looped Reasoning (2604.11791), Stabilizing Recurrent Dynamics (2605.26733), Ouroboros (2604.02051); Survey on Latent Reasoning (2507.06203). **SURGING.** Freshness HIGH (architecture-level reframing of CoT). Paired CRITIQUE: "Do Latent Tokens Think?" (2512.21711) says latent tokens = compressed pattern-matching, less robust than claimed → contested, not validated.
- **A2. Scaling laws for inference / test-time compute (formalized)**. **"Test-Time Scaling Makes Overtraining Compute-Optimal"** (2604.01411, Apr 2026, Wisconsin+Stanford) — T² (Train-to-Test) laws; once inference priced in, compute-optimal model is SMALLER + more overtrained than Chinchilla; validated by pretraining in predicted regime. Also 2602.01485 (reward tail-guided search). Lineage: Snell et al. ICLR 2025 Oral; 2408.00724. **SURGING (high-prestige, oral-tier).** Freshness HIGH ("inference-aware scaling laws" reverses Chinchilla).
- **A3. Generative "thinking" process reward models / verifiers**. **ThinkPRM** (2504.16828, ICLR 2026) generative long-CoT verifier beats discriminative PRMs w/ 100x less data; **GenPRM** (AAAI 2026) 1.5B > GPT-4o on ProcessBench; Learning Generative Selection for BoN (2602.02143); survey 2510.08049. **STEADY→SURGING.** Freshness MED (PRMs old; generative/test-time-scaled verifier is fresh; some hype risk).
- **A4. The efficiency inversion: overthinking, thinking-budgets, fixed-cost reasoning**. "When More Thinking Hurts" (2604.10739), "Fewer Tokens More Accuracy" (2604.21764), "Think Deep Not Just Long" (2602.13517), ESTAR (2602.10004), Efficient Reasoning at Fixed Cost (2603.09253), Breaking the Overscaling Curse (2601.21619). **INSTITUTIONAL PROXY: NeurIPS 2025 First Workshop on Efficient Reasoning = 1,000+ attendees, 275 submissions** + NeurIPS 2025 tutorial on test-time compute. **SURGING (highest volume).** Freshness MED-HIGH (inverts o1/R1 "longer is better").
- **A5. CoT faithfulness / unfaithfulness (monitorability stakes)**. Anthropic "Reasoning Models Don't Always Say What They Think" (2505.05410); 2026 escalation: Systematic Underreporting in CoT (2601.00830), Why Models Know But Don't Say (2603.26410), Decoding Answers Before CoT (2603.01437). **STEADY, broadening** (reasoning ∩ safety/interp). Freshness MED.
- **A6. Reasoning beyond math/code (rubric/reference rewards)**. RLVRR (2601.18533, ICLR 2026); R2-Write (2604.03004); Med-U1 (2602.09305); Alternating RL rubric (2602.01511). **STEADY (fragmenting by domain).**
- **A-skeptic.** "The Illusion of Thinking" (Apple 2506.06941) + rebuttals (2506.09250, 2507.01231); "On the Notion that LMs Reason" (2511.11810). Use to calibrate hype.

## NeurIPS 2026 prediction candidates
- **B1 Latent/looped (recurrent-depth) reasoning = recognized cluster + paired critique track.** Novel (architecture-level, depth-as-scaling-axis). High-prob (Ouro anchor + ≥5 Feb–May 2026 follow-ons = canonical pre-conf flood; critique guarantees contestation→attention). **Conf 78%.** Criterion: ≥8 main-track papers on looped/recurrent-depth/latent reasoning AND ≥1 of {spotlight/oral, invited talk, workshop session}.
- **B2 Inference-aware scaling laws (test-time compute in the scaling-law formalism).** Novel (a LAW reversing Chinchilla, not empirical curve). High-prob (2604.01411, 2602.01485; ICLR 2025 Oral lineage; scaling-law papers win orals). **Conf 70% theme prominent / ~45% a single paper hits spotlight+.** Criterion: ≥1 paper formalizing inference/test-time scaling laws + ≥3 citing/extending T².
- **B3 Efficient reasoning / overthinking / thinking-budgets = first-class objective.** Novel (inverts "longer is better"). High-prob (highest volume + 1,000-person NeurIPS 2025 workshop proxy). **Conf 82% (highest).** Criterion: NeurIPS 2026 "Efficient Reasoning" workshop recurs OR ≥15 main-track papers centering reasoning efficiency/overthinking/budgets.
- **B4 Generative "thinking" PRMs / test-time-scaled verifiers.** Novel (verifier becomes a reasoner spending inference compute). High-prob (ThinkPRM ICLR2026, GenPRM AAAI2026). **Conf 65%.** Criterion: ≥6 papers on generative/CoT PRMs or test-time-scaled verifiers.
- **B5 CoT faithfulness/monitorability = prominent reasoning-safety thread.** Novel (trust/monitorability angle). Mod prob (Anthropic anchor + open-weight 2026 reps). **Conf 55%.** Criterion: ≥5 papers on reasoning-model CoT faithfulness/monitorability OR safety-workshop feature.

**Skeptic bottom line:** dominant risk = baseline absorption (reasoning so pervasive no sub-thread reads as distinct). B3 safest (volume+workshop). B1 highest-novelty/variance (contested by 2512.21711). B2 prestige play (few papers, oral profile).
