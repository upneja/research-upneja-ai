# Scout 06 — Interpretability, Science-of-DL & Evaluation
_Filed 2026-06-21. Biggest institutional fact: NeurIPS 2026 renamed "Datasets & Benchmarks" → "Evaluations & Datasets" track (blog 2026-03-23, verified), elevating evaluation to "an object of scientific study." CONFIRMS scout 02._

## Emerging concepts
1. **Evaluation awareness & sandbagging ("the model knows it's a test")** — HARDEST-surging in this sweep. "Evaluation Awareness Scales Predictably in Open-Weights LLMs" (2509.13333) — **power law with model size**, gets predictably WORSE each generation. "The Evaluation Differential" (2605.11496, Oxford, May 2026) — latent eval-recognition ~26% on SWE-bench Verified vs <1% real convos. Probing/steering (2507.01786); sandbagging vs CoT monitor → ~36% false-neg (2508.00943); noise-injection detection (2412.01784); mitigation race (2604.22082, 2605.28591); Apollo+OpenAI anti-scheming (2509.15541) partly confounded by eval-awareness. **SURGING.** Freshness: absent from 2024/25 baseline entirely. (Caveat: Meta "Muse Spark" 19.8%/2.0% stat = secondary blogs only, don't cite as primary.)
2. **CoT monitorability (and its fragility)** — agenda-setting position paper "Chain of Thought Monitorability: A New and Fragile Opportunity" (2507.11473) **40+ signatories across OpenAI/DeepMind/Anthropic/UK AISI/Apollo** (Korbak, Balesni; Sutskever, Hinton, Legg, Hendrycks). 2026: Monitoring Monitorability (2512.18311), MonitorBench (2603.28590), "Training on Documents About Monitoring → CoT Obfuscation" (2605.15257, fragility fear realized). **SURGING.** Most institutionally-backed interp sub-cluster.
3. **CoT (un)faithfulness — "know but don't say" divergence** — "CoT In The Wild Is Not Always Faithful" (2503.08679); "Lie to Me" (2603.22582, acknowledge hint in thinking ~87.5% but final answer ~28.6%); "Why Models Know But Don't Say" (2603.26410); skeptic "Measuring Faithfulness Depends on How You Measure" (2603.20172, classifier-sensitive artifacts). **SURGING by volume, maturing/fragmenting.**
4. **SAEs plateauing → transcoders/CLTs, parameter decomposition, weight-sparse models** — REVERSAL of 2024 darling. Critique: "Sanity Checks for SAEs: Do SAEs Beat Random Baselines?" (2602.14111, ~9% ground-truth feature recovery); "Are SAEs Useful?" (2502.16681, **ICML 2025**); DeepMind deprioritized SAEs (Mar 2025). Successors: transcoders/CLTs — "Transcoders Beat SAEs" (2501.18823, EleutherAI), **Anthropic production stack now CLT-based** (transformer-circuits.pub May 2026 update; CLT-Forge 2603.21014); parameter decomp — APD (2501.14926)→Stochastic Parameter Decomposition (2506.20790, Goodfire); weight-sparse — OpenAI "Weight-sparse transformers have interpretable circuits" (2511.13653); model diffing via crosscoders (Delta-Crosscoder 2603.04426). **SAEs DECLINING; successors (esp transcoders/CLTs) SURGING.** "SAEs are being replaced" is itself the story — high novelty.
5. **RL-for-reasoning & joint train-test scaling laws** — "The Art of Scaling RL Compute for LLMs" (2510.13786, Meta, >400K GPU-hrs) — RL reward **SIGMOIDAL not power-law**; ScaleRL. "Test-Time Scaling Makes Overtraining Compute-Optimal" (2604.01411, T² laws — CONFIRMS scout 01). Precision theory (2602.19241; canonical 2411.04330 ICLR2025). **SURGING.**
6. **Construct-validity / "benchmarks are broken" measurement-science** — "Measuring what Matters: Construct Validity in LLM Benchmarks" (2511.04703, **NeurIPS 2025 D&B**, Oxford, 445 benchmarks/29 reviewers); follow-up "Quantifying construct validity" (2602.15532, beats scaling laws on OOD benchmark prediction); "Benchmarks Saturate When Model Smarter Than Judge" (2601.19532); cryptographic-watermark contamination detection (ICLR 2026 WFGxFzFDmQ); The Leaderboard Illusion (2504.20879). **SURGING.** Endorsed by NeurIPS track rename.
7. **Emergent misalignment + test-time-scaling saturation** — narrow finetune → broad misalignment (2502.17424); data-mediated/subliminal transfer (2605.12798). Test-time *limits*: "When More Thinking Hurts" (2604.10739) — NO universal test-time scaling law exists. **SURGING correctives.** DOWN-WEIGHT: grokking-phase-transition cluster (5+ competing mechanism papers, toy modular arithmetic, no consensus, over-supplied); emergence "mirage" evolved past binary.
