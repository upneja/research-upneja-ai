# Scout 04 — Generative Models Beyond Autoregressive Text
_Filed 2026-06-21. Verdicts: SURGING / STEADY / HYPE-ONLY._

## Emerging concepts
1. **Diffusion Language Models (dLLMs)** — masked/discrete diffusion generating text by parallel denoising. Evidence: Mercury (arXiv 2506.17298) GA on Bedrock/SageMaker/Azure AI Foundry; **Mercury 2** (2026-02-24) "first reasoning dLLM" ~1000 tok/s; **LLaDA 2.0: Scaling to 100B** (2512.15745); RL-for-dLLM subfield: d2 (2509.21474), diffu-GRPO/d1, Group Diffusion Policy Optimization (2510.08554), DiFFPO (2510.02212), DMPO (2510.08233); Dream 7B / Dream-Coder 7B, Seed Diffusion (2508.02193); Survey (2508.10875); ICLR 2026 accepted ParallelBench (2510.04767), AdaBlock-dLLM. **SURGING.** Fresh axis = diffusion for *text/reasoning/code* (not images).
2. **World Models for agent training** ("learn inside imagination", neural game engines). Evidence: **Dreamer 4 — Training Agents Inside of Scalable World Models** (2509.24527, Sep 2025) — 2B params, Minecraft diamonds from offline data, RL fully inside learned WM, 21 FPS/1 GPU, 100x less data than VPT; DeepMind **Genie 3** (Aug 2025, 24fps/720p), Project Genie (2026-01-29), **Waymo World Model** (Feb 2026); **World Labs Marble** (Fei-Fei Li, persistent 3D worlds); V-JEPA 2/2.1; new 2026: VideoWorld 2 (2602.10102), Kairos (2606.16533); **dedicated ICLR 2026 Workshop on World Models**; ~$1B+ capital (World Labs, AMI Labs). **SURGING** — arguably hottest non-text theme of 2026.
3. **One-step / few-step generation via flow maps (MeanFlow lineage)** — generate in 1 NFE by learning average velocity, no distillation. Evidence: **MeanFlow** (2505.13447, Kaiming He) FID 3.43 @1-NFE ImageNet-256 from scratch; **Pixel MeanFlow** (2601.22158) 2.22 FID, latent-free; AlphaFlow (2510.20771), FACM (2507.03738), SplitMeanFlow (2507.16884); Dreamer 4's "shortcut forcing" = few-step gen inside a WM (threads converging). **SURGING.**
4. **Flow matching / rectified flow as default objective** — displaced DDPM score-matching; substrate of SD3, FLUX, Veo; FLUX.2 = "rectified flow matching"; ICML 2026 tutorial "Diffusion and Flow-Matching"; Discrete Flow Matching extending to discrete data (2509.21912). **STEADY** (already won; now infrastructure). Best treated as connective substrate, not standalone prediction.
5. **Unified multimodal (understanding AND generation in one backbone)** — Evidence: **BAGEL** (2505.14683) 88% GenEval; Janus-Pro (2501.17811); 2026 wave (Representation Forcing 2605.31604, BLIP3o-NEXT 2510.15857, "Unified Multimodal Models as Auto-Encoders" 2509.09666); industry: **GPT-Image-2** (2026-04-21), Nano Banana Pro / Gemini 3 Pro Image (reasoning-infused gen). **SURGING (research) / partly HYPE.** Durable signal = autoregressive-reasoning + diffusion-rendering hybrid.

## NeurIPS 2026 prediction candidates
- **P1 World models for agent training = marquee theme.** Novel (generative-model-as-environment, not text agents). High-prob (Dreamer 4 is this cycle; ICLR 2026 WM workshop; frontier releases + $1B labs). **Conf 88%.** Criterion: NeurIPS 2026 hosts WM workshop AND/OR ≥8 main-track papers w/ "world model" in title on agent training/planning, AND/OR a WM spotlight/oral/award.
- **P2 dLLMs = recognized main-track cluster w/ reasoning/RL sub-theme.** Novel (diffusion moves into text/reasoning). High-prob (Mercury GA, LLaDA 2.0 100B, RL-for-dLLM literature; ICLR 2026 already accepted dLLM papers). **Conf 82%.** Criterion: ≥10 NeurIPS 2026 accepted dLLM papers AND/OR a dLLM workshop/tutorial AND/OR ≥1 spotlight/oral. Risk: dLLMs may under-index at NeurIPS vs ICLR/ICML.
- **P3 One-step flow-map generation (MeanFlow lineage) = named contested sub-area.** Novel (distillation-free few-step, average-velocity reframing; efficiency engine enabling P1/P2). High-prob (MeanFlow + Pixel MeanFlow + 2026 follow-ups; Kaiming He track record). **Conf 72%.** Criterion: ≥5 NeurIPS 2026 papers building on MeanFlow/flow-map one-step gen (citing 2505.13447) AND/OR a spotlight/oral.
- **P4 Unified understanding+generation as labeled track.** Novel (co-equal generation; reasoning-then-render). High-prob (BAGEL/Janus-Pro + dense 2026 stream; GPT-Image-2, Nano Banana Pro). **Conf 70%.** Criterion: ≥8 NeurIPS 2026 unified-multimodal papers AND/OR dedicated workshop/tutorial. Caveat: many incremental; "unify everything" partly hype.

**Scout meta:** Strongest single bet = P1 (world models). Most novel-yet-grounded = P3 (one-step flow maps). Flow matching itself = substrate, too easy to be a useful standalone forecast.
